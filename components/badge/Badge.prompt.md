Small pill label for trust signals, status indicators, and count markers.

```jsx
<Badge variant="trust" icon="🔒">Pago seguro</Badge>
<Badge variant="trust">Sorteo ante notario</Badge>
<Badge variant="success">Participación confirmada</Badge>
<Badge variant="warning">Correo puede tardar</Badge>
<Badge variant="neutral">Transmisión en vivo</Badge>
```

Variants: `trust` (gold), `success`, `warning`, `danger`, `info`, `neutral`.
Always uppercase — letter-spacing applied automatically. Use for trust-bar badges, status pills, and count chips.
